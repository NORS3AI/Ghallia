# Ghallia - Feature Overview

> A comprehensive list of features for stakeholders and investors.

---

## Game Concept

Ghallia is a **hybrid idle/active crafting game** that combines the satisfying progression of RuneScape with the accessibility of modern idle games. Players actively gather resources through engaging click-based gameplay, then passively craft and sell items while away.

---

## Core Features

### Hybrid Gameplay Model
- **Active Gathering** - Engaging click/tap mechanics for resource collection
- **Idle Crafting** - Queue-based crafting that progresses while offline
- **Best of Both Worlds** - Active players are rewarded, but progress continues when away
- **Mobile-Friendly** - Designed for both desktop and mobile play

### Massive Skill System
- **20 Unique Skills** spanning gathering, crafting, and support categories
- **999 Levels Per Skill** - Deep progression with meaningful milestones
- **Skill Unlock Progression** - Strategic choices on which skills to unlock
- **Interconnected Skills** - Gathering feeds crafting; support enhances both

### Prestige & Replayability
- **Prestige System** - Reset progress for powerful permanent bonuses
- **Chaos Points Currency** - Earned through prestige, spent on talents
- **Talent Tree** - Vast customization options for playstyle optimization
- **Infinite Scaling** - Multiple prestige tiers for endless progression

---

## Skill Categories

### Gathering Skills (7 Skills)
Active, click-based resource collection:

- **Logging** - Chop trees for wood (free starter skill)
- **Mining** - Extract ores, gems, and precious metals
- **Fishing** - Catch fish from rivers, lakes, and oceans
- **Herbalism** - Gather herbs, flowers, and medicinal plants
- **Skinning** - Harvest leather, hides, and animal materials
- **Foraging** - Collect wild ingredients and rare finds
- **Hunting** - Track and capture wild game for materials

### Crafting Skills (9 Skills)
Idle, queue-based production:

- **Sawmill** - Process wood into lumber, planks, and furniture
- **Smithing** - Forge weapons, armor, and metal goods
- **Cooking** - Prepare food items with stat buffs
- **Alchemy** - Brew potions, elixirs, and transmutations
- **Leatherworking** - Craft leather armor, bags, and accessories
- **Tailoring** - Create cloth armor, robes, and containers
- **Jewelcrafting** - Cut gems and create valuable jewelry
- **Enchanting** - Imbue items with magical properties
- **Engineering** - Build gadgets, tools, and mechanical devices

### Support Skills (4 Skills)
Passive bonuses unlocked at Prestige 1:

- **Trading** - Better buy/sell prices at merchants
- **Farming** - Passive crop and herb generation
- **Runecrafting** - Create magical runes for enchanting
- **Archaeology** - Discover rare artifacts and ancient blueprints

## Progression System

### Skill Unlocking
- **Free Starter** - Logging available immediately at no cost
- **Guided First Purchase** - Sawmill unlocks the crafting loop (1,000g)
- **Player Choice** - Subsequent skills unlocked in any order
- **Scaling Costs** - 10,000g → 50,000g → 100,000g and beyond
- **Strategic Decisions** - Players choose their own progression path

### Leveling System
- **Level Range**: 1 to 999 per skill
- **Exponential XP Curve** - Early levels fast, late levels prestigious
- **Milestone Rewards** - Bonuses at key level thresholds
- **Prestige Requirement** - 5 skills at level 99+ to unlock prestige

### Prestige Mechanics
- **Chaos Points** - Premium currency earned by prestiging
- **Full Reset** - Gold, levels, and items reset on prestige
- **Scaling Rewards** - More Chaos Points for higher achievements
- **Support Skill Unlock** - Prestige 1 reveals 4 powerful support skills

## Talent System

### Talent Categories
Spend Chaos Points on permanent, account-wide bonuses:

- **Skill XP Boosts** - Faster leveling in specific skill categories
- **Gold Multipliers** - Increased earnings from all sales
- **Crafting Speed** - Reduced crafting times
- **Multi-Craft** - Chance to produce multiple items per craft
- **Resource Efficiency** - Lower material requirements for recipes
- **Rare Finds** - Higher chance for rare materials while gathering
- **Idle Efficiency** - Improved offline progression rates
- **Prestige Bonuses** - Earn more Chaos Points per prestige

### Talent Tree Design
- **Multiple Branches** - Specialize in gathering, crafting, or balance both
- **Synergy Nodes** - Powerful bonuses for specific skill combinations
- **Prestige-Gated Tiers** - Higher prestige unlocks deeper talent tiers
- **Respec Options** - Ability to reallocate Chaos Points

## Stat System

### Primary Stats
| Stat | Effect | Primary Source |
|------|--------|----------------|
| **Strength** | +% Tap Power per point | Plate/Mail Armor, Weapons |
| **Intellect** | +% Mana Regeneration per point | Cloth Armor |
| **Agility** | +% Luck and Dodge per point | Leather Armor |
| **Stamina** | +HP per point (base 10 HP) | All armor types, Jewelry |

### Stat Calculations
- **Tap Power Bonus**: Strength × 1% per point
- **Mana Regen Bonus**: Intellect × 2% per point
- **Luck Bonus**: Agility × 0.5% per point
- **Dodge Chance**: Agility × 0.25% per point
- **Max HP**: 10 + (Stamina × 5)

## Crafting Skill Details

- **Products**: Leather versions of all armor slots
- **Materials**: Hides from Skinning
- **Primary Stats**: Agility, Stamina

- **Products**: Robes, Hoods, Cloth versions of all slots, Cloaks

- **Primary Stats**: Intellect, Stamina

### Jewelcrafting (Accessories)
- **Products**: Rings, Necklaces, Trinkets

## Foraging Special Mechanics

### Scavenging System
Foraging is unique - it provides random finds from all categories:

- **Random Resources**: Small amounts of wood, ore, fish, herbs
- **Scrap Materials**: Metal scraps for Smithing, cloth scraps for Tailoring
- **Equipment Drops**: Rare chance to find weapons and armor
  - Mostly low-tier (Copper, Bronze, Iron)
  - Extremely rare high-tier finds (Dragon gear = jackpot!)
- **Quality varies by Foraging level**

### Foraging Drop Table
| Category | Chance | Notes |
|----------|--------|-------|
| Common Resources | 50% | Random wood, ore, fish, herbs |
| Scrap Materials | 30% | Smithing/Tailoring crafting materials |
| Equipment (Common) | 15% | Copper-Silver tier gear |
| Equipment (Rare+) | 4% | Mithril-Platinum tier gear |
| Equipment (Legendary) | 1% | Diamond-Dragon tier gear |
